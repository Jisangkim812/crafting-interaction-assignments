Overview

This is a personal case study project that aims to share various environmental information  and activities in an engaging and enjoyable way. The project includes easy and fun missions designed to spark interest in the environment.

Outcome

Home

Based on the environmental activities you are interested in, you can see photos of other users' activities at a glance, and group activities are also encouraged to take on challenges with other users.

Challenge (Gamification)

By providing missions/quests in a Gamification, I planned UX to make changes in users' daily lives energetic and fun.

Community

I designed a community space so that users with the same interests could freely share environmental information that can not be easily obtained elsewhere.

My Page

This is a space where users can view the badges they have earned through completing missions and see their environmental activities.
